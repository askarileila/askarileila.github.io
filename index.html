Currently I work as a consultant & engineer at Capgemini Engineering. I am a recent PhD graduate in Information Technology. During my PhD my main research topic involved dynamic placement of Virtual Network Functions (VNFs) for service function chain provisioning.I had the opportunity to be involved in METRO-HAUL (EU Horizon 2020 Project) contributing in different work packages. I have more than four years hands on experience as network administrator, being responsible for managing Linux and Windows servers, Cisco switches and Mikrotik routers. I am eager to learn and ready to accept challenges.
